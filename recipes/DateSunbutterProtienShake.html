Date Sunbutter Protien Shake

This shake is deceptively delicious. The sunbutter is sour as sea water; the dates add a sweet earthy foam.

Ingredients

    Dry Ingredients

  • 40g of one-minute oats
  • 2 tablespoons of sunbutter
  • 13g of ground flaxseeds
  • 2-3 pitted Medjool dates

    Wet Ingredients

  • 1 cup of organic whole milk

    Toppings

  • pinch of cinnamon
  • pinch of nutmeg

Steps

  1. Add dry ingredients and toppings to Nutribullet
  2. Add wet ingredients.
  3. Blend until light and smooth.
  4. Stir and enjoy!
